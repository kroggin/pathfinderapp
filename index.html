How it works

Guide, don't give

Unlike AI tools that just hand over answers, Pathfinder uses the Socratic method — asking questions that lead your child to discover answers themselves.

1

Student asks a question

Your child types their homework question, a problem they're stuck on, or a concept they don't understand.

2

Pathfinder guides with questions

Instead of giving the answer, the AI tutor asks guiding questions that help the student work through the problem step by step.

3

Student discovers the answer

The student reaches the answer themselves — building real understanding, not just copying. You see their progress in the parent dashboard.
